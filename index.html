What is VOXEL3?

I've designed VOXEL3 as a Language Model that has some improvements over a traditional model specifically in the Khmer language, making it accessible for those with limited knowledge in the English language.

Additionally, this model is dedicated to teaching Cambodian students particularly in the National Exam "Baccalaurate II"
